Walk To The Match

Join the club's Walk to the Match campaign — whether you are walking to the Wham Stadium or your local grassroots facility!

Let's Move Hyndburn is a collaboration between Accrington Stanley Football Club, Community Trust, Supporters' Trust, and Women's Football Club, inspiring people to be more active by walking to the Stanley match.

It all began at the Stanley and Derby County match, when fans came together for the campaign launch. Director David Burgess quoted: "Let's keep walking to the game in numbers."
